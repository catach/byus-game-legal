Contact us:
dbugsbase-byugame@yahoo.com
We typically respond within a few business days.

When emailing us, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • The version of Byu's Game you're using
  • A clear description of the issue or question
  • Screenshots, if applicable

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Byu's Game collect any of my data?

No. Byu's Game does not collect, store, or share any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The game is crashing or not working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, restart your device, then make sure you have the latest version installed from the App Store. If the problem persists, email us with the details above.

How do I reset my game progress?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling and reinstalling the app will reset all progress.

Will my progress transfer to a new device?

Since we do not collect or store any data on our servers, progress stays on the device where you play. Game progress does not transfer between devices.

How can I share feedback or suggest features?

We'd love to hear from you! Send your thoughts to the email above.
