Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Byu's Game ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of our mobile application available on the Apple App Store.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information or user data.

Byu's Game does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data from its users. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or location data.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or crash reporting services.
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or any other personal data on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

Any game progress, settings, or preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party. This data remains under your control and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

Byu's Game does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data, including analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Byu's Game does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no data from any users, this applies to all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
